Teaching Philosophy

My teaching emphasizes connecting theory with practical applications in insurance and finance. I encourage students to build strong foundations in probability and statistics while developing problem-solving skills for actuarial practice.

Mentorship

I supervise graduate and undergraduate students interested in actuarial science, insurance, and risk management, and actively encourage students to participate in research projects and professional development opportunities.
