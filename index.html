About

I am an Assistant Professor of Actuarial Science in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at York University. My research focuses on principal–agent problems in (re)insurance design, risk management in finance and insurance, and the design of modern insurance products such as variable annuities. I also study emerging risks such as cyber and AI-driven risks.

Teaching

  • ACTG 4281 – Life Contingencies
  • Graduate Supervision – PhD research in risk management and actuarial science
